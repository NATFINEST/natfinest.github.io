FCMB Formally Unveils Women In Business Proposition

In commemoration of International Women’s Day, on Thursda7, March 7, 2019, FCMB hosted a “Women with Vision” Gala to celebrate women from all walks of life. The event held at the Federal Palace Hotel, Victoria Island and was anchored by two A-list female celebrities, Helen Paul and Osas Ajibade.

The major highlight of the event was the formal unveiling of “SheVentures”, the FCMB women in business proposition. The unveil was done by the Managing Director of FCMB, Mr. Adam Nuru; Executive Director, Finance, Mrs. Yemisi Edun; Executive Director, Business Development, Mrs. Bukola Smith and Founder, WISCAR, Mrs. Aminat Oyagbola amongst others. This was followed by the presentation of cheques to the first set of beneficiaries of the SheVentures Zero Interest Loan.

Beyond the thrills and excitement, there was an educative panel session, which was quite interactive with questions and answers from panelists and members of the audience.
