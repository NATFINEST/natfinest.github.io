AGRIC BUSINESS

With various agriculture and its value chain businesses, structured trade and commodity that has been supported by FCMB Agric business, this has made us a bank of choice for Agric value chains customers by promoting and financing their business and offer sound advisory services when needed.

JOIN FORUM

We support various players in Agric value chain such as

  • Input providers: Fertilizer, Agro Chemicals, Animal Feed manufacturers and suppliers, seed providers, farm equipment manufacturers, suppliers or service providers.
  • Farmers: Small holder to large scale farmers producing annual crops, plantations and Livestock such as poultry, cattle, goats, rams, pigs and fisheries.
  • Agro Dealers: buyers and aggregators of farm produce, traders of farm produce and livestock.
  • Agro Processors: Processors of farm grains e.g. tender millers, Oil Palm processors, soya bean processors, Cassava, rice, Sesame seeds processing, livestock processing and packaging.
  • Industrial manufactures: e.g. Cassava syrup, Starch, ethanol.
  • Trade and Exports: e.g. Local commodity traders, Exporters.

We have a robust engagement with the below government financial institutions in making various government intervention funds at single digit rate available to support the above-mentioned Agric value chains.

  • CBN - Central Bank of Nigeria
  • BOA - Bank of Agriculture

  • DBN - Development bank of Nigeria

We also have Agro-Commodity Trade Finance which is targeted at the Agro-commodity merchants who supply grains and cash crops such as rice, maize, sorghum, soybean, wheat, ginger, cocoa, sesame, cashew, etc. to urban wholesalers, exporters and major processing/manufacturing companies in Nigeria.

We held and supported several events in 2018 and 2019 as stated below

2018 Agritech Startup Investor - Wennovation Hub in partnership with FCMB announced the 2018 AgriTech Incubation Program and called ideas in Agri-finance/insurance, procurement and supply chain management, and other Agri-ICT areas. After an intense two-week review process, we selected 10 startups out of 198 total applications from across all 6 Geo-political zones for the incubation program. The Wennovation Hub incubation program is set to help early stage entrepreneurs validate their ideas/markets, build sustainable business models and attract follow-on investments through a combination of peer-learning, mentorship and seed capital.

Tractor Programme - The tractor programme is in collaboration with Tractor Owners & Hiring Facilities Association of Nigeria (TOHFAN), Traxi Nigeria Limited, Tractor Owners and Operators Association of Nigeria (TOOAN), Tractor Vendors (John Deer, Tak Tractors, Niji Tractors etc.) and Propcom Mar-Karfi. So far, over 500 tractors have been financed under a Lease Finance Scheme and distributed to selected and trained members of TOHFAN and TOOAN.
