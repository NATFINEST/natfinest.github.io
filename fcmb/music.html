About FCMB Music

The FCMB Music is a platform for Nigerian Musical Creatives (Artists, Producers, Record Labels and Songwriters) to set-up a consolidated mobile profile that includes all their studio quality songs, artwork, and lyrics. Their profile will have social media and Short Code integration which will build their database of fans and followers around the globe. Their FCMB MUSIC profile will enable them to establish intellectual property rights for their musical creations via a major American Performance Rights Organization (PRO).

FCMB Music has an integrated and comprehensive online marketing package offering these items:

  • Online Store in FCMB Music Mall
  • Social Media Scheme
  • Fan Club
  • Ability to sell merchandise
  • Social Media, Email and Text Marketing
  • Caller Ringtones
  • Regular, VIP and Meet and Greet Levels
