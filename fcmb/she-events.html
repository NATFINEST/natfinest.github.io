FCMB Formally Unveils Women In Business Proposition

NIWEX EXHIBITION

NIWEX is an international exhibition organized annually by Nigeria Association of Chamber of commerce, Industry, Mines and Agriculture Business Women Group (NAWORG) in conjunction with Atlantic Exhibition Nigeria Limited and partnership with Ecowas Federation of Business Women Entrepreneur (FEBWE), Uplift Development Foundation and Women’s Radio Wfm 91.7.

11 - 12 Oct. 2018

NECA’S Network Of Entrepreneurial Women (NNEW) 2018 Summit

NNNEW is a platform established in 2005 under the aegis of Nigeria Employer’s Consultative Association to promote and nurture entrepreneurship amongst women. The Network holds yearly summits where issues surrounding women-owned businesses are x-rayed, discussed and enhanced for growth.
