Competitive Sales Strategies

In the summer of 2016, our office at JA Africa received a barrage of requests from students across Ghana looking for opportunities to join the organization as interns; to learn and to contribute towards a mission they care about. I was impressed by the quality of applications that we received and the assertiveness with which they were sent. In the end, after an extensive process we hired three interns. Our office is small, with very little room for everyone, and we have been intentional about cultivating a team culture which is inclusive and fun so we get to know each other well and quickly. We also learn a great deal from each other.
